Clinical, research & teaching activities

1. Public Health & Preventive Medicine Practice

Contributed to national health promotion and disease prevention initiatives, focusing on workplace health programs and epidemiologic surveillance. Supported national livestock worker health checks (2022) and proposed reforms to the 36-hour consecutive duty system for medical residents (2023). Gained practical experience in national mental health policy and service programs through secondment to the National Center for Mental Health (2023); Secondment, Department of Mental Health Services, National Center for Mental Health Jan 2023 – Feb 2023.

2. Research in Public Health Medicine

Research social determinants of health, public mental health, and workplace well-being. Led or contributed to studies on medical residents’ duty hours, COVID-19 and health equity, and spatiotemporal disease analyses. Presented work at conferences by the Korean Society for Preventive Medicine, KAHPM, and KAMS.

3. General Clinical Practice

Gained clinical experience across primary care, emergency medicine, and post-operative care. Served as a patient care physician at Seoul’s Community Treatment Centre during the COVID-19 pandemic (2022); Physician, Seoul COVID-19 Community Treatment Center (part-time) Jan 2022 – Jun 2022.

4. Education & Teaching

As a teaching assistant at Korea University College of Medicine, support courses in Preventive Medicine, Medical Law, and community-based clinical electives. Organize Preventive Medicine Grand Rounds to foster interactive learning and strengthen graduate-level training.

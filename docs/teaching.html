Syllabus — Korean Healthcare Policy 101 (16 weeks)

  • Week 1 — Origins and contradictions of Korea’s health system: is the National Health Insurance system, expected to face deficits faster than the national pension, sustainable?
  • Week 2 — Introduction to comparative healthcare systems: international comparison of major systems and health indicators.
  • Weeks 3–4 — National Health Insurance (financing, risk pooling, fee schedules, payment systems) & Long-Term Care Insurance.
  • Week 5 — Healthcare system ①: primary care, chronic-disease management, attending-physician system, integrated care.
  • Week 6 — Healthcare system ②: emergency medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics, critical care (“essential medical services”).
  • Weeks 7–8 — Healthcare system ③: public healthcare, health inequalities, regional healthcare, social policy/environments & health, catastrophic health expenditure.
  • Week 9 — Healthcare system ④: suicide prevention, public mental health, mental-healthcare system.
  • Week 10 — Healthcare system ⑤: infectious-disease & disaster response, global health, unification healthcare, military healthcare system.
  • Week 11 — Health workforce policy ①: hospital workforce structure, medical education & training (PGME, CME), working conditions, Medical Residents Act, Nursing Act, supply/demand projections (medical-school quotas).
  • Week 12 — Health workforce policy ②: physician assistants (PA), scope-of-practice adjustment, medical unification, medical disputes & legal liability, physician/nurse immigration.
  • Week 13 — Health workforce policy ③: healthcare workers’ unions — major activities and policy issues (industrial action, mass resignation of residents).
  • Week 14 — Healthcare industry, regulation, and legislative policy.
  • Week 15 — The policy process for practitioners: National Assembly, government, and civil society.
  • Week 16 — Conclusion & assessment: future challenges and directions / (graduate) final examination.
국문 주차별 상세
  • 1주차 — 대한민국 보건의료체계의 기원과 모순: 연금보다 빠른 적자가 예상되는 전국민 건강보험제도, 지속 가능한가?
  • 2주차 — 비교보건의료체계 총론: 각국 주요제도 및 건강지표 국제 비교
  • 3~4주차 — 국민건강보험 총론(재정조달, 위험분담, 수가체계, 지불제도 등) 및 장기요양보험 총론
  • 5주차 — 보건의료체계 ①: 일차보건의료, 만성질환 관리, 주치의제, 통합돌봄
  • 6주차 — 보건의료체계 ②: 응급의료, 소아, 분만, 중환자 (소위 필수의료)
  • 7~8주차 — 보건의료체계 ③: 공공보건의료, 건강불평등, 지역의료, 사회정책/사회환경과 건강, 재난적 의료비
  • 9주차 — 보건의료체계 ④: 자살예방, 공중정신보건, 정신보건의료체계
  • 10주차 — 보건의료체계 ⑤: 감염병 및 재난 대응, 국제보건, 통일보건의료, 군 보건의료체계
  • 11주차 — 의료인력정책 ①: 병원 인력 구조, 의료인 교육수련(PGME, CME), 노동조건 및 처우, 전공의법, 간호법, 수급 추계 (의대정원 등)
  • 12주차 — 의료인력정책 ②: PA, 직역 간 업무 범위 조정, 의료일원화, 의료분쟁 및 법적 책임, 의사/간호사 이민
  • 13주차 — 의료인력정책 ③: 보건의료인 노동조합 — 주요 활동 및 정책이슈 (보건의료노조 쟁의행위, 전공의 대규모 사직 사태 등)
  • 14주차 — 헬스케어 산업, 규제, 입법정책
  • 15주차 — 정책실무자에게 필요한 정책과정 이해: 국회, 정부, 시민사회
